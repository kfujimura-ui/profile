From industry to academia

Profile

Designing learning across industry and academia.

Before 2019, he served as Representative Director and Executive Officer at private English education companies, contributing to global talent development through ESP consulting for a wide range of organizations.

Current Focus

Current Research Themes

His expertise includes ESP and the development and assessment of active learning-based curricula. Through practices such as MUN, OISC, and PBL, he explores the Meeting-Style Classroom and Willingness to Communicate, while also examining what kinds of AI use are most beneficial for learners within that process.

Academic Roles

Current Positioning

In addition to his role as Associate Professor at Aichi Institute of Technology, he has served as an Adjunct Lecturer in the Project-based English Program at Ritsumeikan University since April 2025. He also taught in the GLIP program at Tokyo University of Foreign Studies from October 2022 to March 2025.
